## Part 1: Veo3 Video Generation for E-commerce

### What is Veo3?

Google **Veo 3** is an advanced AI video generator that can create:
- Cinematic product videos
- Viral branding content
- TikTok-ready ecommerce ads
- AI influencer content

### Key Capabilities for TikTok Shop

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Consistent Characters** | Create AI influencers that appear across multiple videos |
| **Product Demos** | Generate demonstrations without expensive video shoots |
| **Canvas Prompting** | Technique to integrate real products into AI-generated videos |
| **Cost Effective** | ~$0.20 per video generation with API access (80% cost reduction) |
| **Viral Content** | Generate UGC-style content that performs well on TikTok |

### Veo3 Prompting Best Practices

When generating videos with Veo3, follow these prompting guidelines:

1. **Specify Platform Format**
   - "Vertical 9:16 format for TikTok"
   - "Mobile-first vertical video"
   - "1080x1920 resolution"

2. **Include Product Details**
   - Be specific about lighting: "soft natural lighting", "studio lighting"
   - Specify camera angles: "close-up shot", "wide angle", "product rotation"
   - Mention movement: "slow motion", "360 degree view", "zoom in on features"

3. **Ground Any Dialogue in Listing Facts**
   - Use the shopping page description/specs as the single source of truth
   - Workflow: `docs/workflows/product-description-to-dialogue-workflow.md`
   - Template: `docs/templates/product-details-sheet.md`

4. **Define Style & Tone**
   - "UGC-style" or "authentic feel" for better engagement
   - "Cinematic quality" for premium products
   - "Casual and friendly" for relatable content

5. **Set Duration**
   - 20-60 seconds recommended for product videos
   - 9-15 seconds for optimal engagement in ads

6. **Example Prompt Structure**
   ```
   "A 30-second vertical TikTok video (9:16) showcasing [PRODUCT NAME].
   Professional studio lighting with soft shadows. Close-up shots showing
   product details. UGC-style authentic presentation. Background: [DESCRIBE].
   Music: upbeat trending style."
   ```

## Part 2: TikTok Shop Video Technical Requirements

### Video Specifications

| Specification | Requirement | Notes |
|---------------|-------------|-------|
| **Resolution** | **1080 x 1920** (9:16 vertical) recommended | Minimum: 540 x 960 |
| **Aspect Ratio** | 9:16 (vertical), 1:1 (square), 16:9 (horizontal) | 9:16 preferred for TikTok Shop |
| **Format** | **MP4** (preferred), MOV, MPEG, 3GP, AVI | MP4 for universal compatibility |
| **Duration** | **20-60 seconds** recommended | Ads: 5-60 seconds (9-15s optimal) |
| **File Size** | Maximum 500 MB | Keep files compressed |
| **Quality** | **720p minimum, 1080p preferred** | HD quality required for product videos |
| **Bitrate** | 2,500 kbps recommended minimum | Higher for better quality |

### Content Requirements for TikTok Shop

1. **Product Visibility**
   - Product must be clearly shown from multiple angles
   - At least 5 high-quality images/videos per product listing
   - Promoted products must match listed products exactly

2. **Safe Zones**
   - Keep important content away from UI overlay areas
   - Top and bottom areas may be covered by TikTok interface

3. **Content Guidelines**
   - Original content only (no reused or copyrighted material)
   - Must be relevant to the linked product
   - Follow TikTok's promotional content guidelines

## Part 3: How to Add Product Links to TikTok Videos

### Prerequisites

Before adding product links, ensure you have:
- [ ] TikTok App installed
- [ ] TikTok **Business Account**
- [ ] **TikTok Shop Seller** or **Affiliate** account
- [ ] Products listed in your TikTok Shop catalog

### Method 1: Adding Product Links During Video Creation

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Create or upload your video in the TikTok app |
| 2 | After editing your video, go to the **Post** screen |
| 3 | Click the **"Add Link"** button |
| 4 | Select **"Products"** from the options |
| 5 | In the Showcase module, click **"Add"** to choose product(s) |
| 6 | Select and edit the product name if needed |
| 7 | Post your video with the product link attached |

### Method 2: Adding Product Links to Existing Videos

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Go to your **TikTok Shop Toolkit** section |
| 2 | Select **"Link Products"** |
| 3 | Scroll through your existing videos |
| 4 | Click **"Add Product"** on the video you want to update |
| 5 | Choose your display option (how product appears) |
| 6 | Select the product from your catalog |
| 7 | Link it and save changes |

### Visual Indicator

When a product is successfully linked, a **yellow shopping basket icon** will appear on your video.

## Part 4: Content Creation Best Practices

### For TikTok Shop Videos

1. **Unveil the Product in Use**
   - Show real product demonstrations
   - Demonstrate how the product solves a problem
   - Show the product in context/real-life scenarios

2. **Keep It Real (Authenticity)**
   - UGC-style content performs better than polished ads
   - Authentic, relatable content builds trust
   - Avoid overly promotional or "salesy" content

3. **Use Trending Formats & Sounds**
   - Leverage current TikTok trends
   - Use trending audio when relevant
   - Adapt popular formats to showcase your product

4. **Storytelling**
   - Create engaging narratives around products
   - Show before/after scenarios
   - Use testimonials or user stories

5. **Multiple Angles**
   - Show product from different perspectives
   - Include close-ups of key features
   - Demonstrate product scale and size

### For Veo3 Generated Content

1. **Combine AI + Real Footage**
   - Use "canvas prompting" to integrate real products
   - Blend AI-generated backgrounds with real product shots
   - Create seamless transitions between AI and real content

2. **Create AI Influencers**
   - Build consistent character-based content
   - Use AI influencer across multiple videos for brand recognition
   - Generate different variations for A/B testing

3. **Product Showcase Techniques**
   - 360-degree product rotation
   - Feature highlights with text overlays
   - Lifestyle settings that match your brand

4. **Variation Generation**
   - Create multiple versions of the same product video
   - Test different styles, backgrounds, and presentations
   - Optimize based on performance data
